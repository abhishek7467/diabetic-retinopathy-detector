About Us

Diabetic Retinopathy Detector is an AI-powered web application designed to help users detect diabetic retinopathy from retinal images. Our mission is to make early detection accessible, fast, and reliable for everyone.

Our Vision

We aim to empower patients, doctors, and clinics with advanced technology to prevent vision loss due to diabetes. Our platform leverages state-of-the-art deep learning models to provide instant, accurate results.
