How to Use

  1. Register for a free account using your email and phone number.
  2. Login to your dashboard.
  3. Go to the Upload page and select a clear retinal image (JPG/PNG).
  4. Click Upload to get an instant AI-powered prediction.
  5. View your results and download the report if needed.
  6. If you forget your password, use the Forgot Password link to reset it via OTP (Email or SMS).

Tips for Best Results

  • Upload high-quality, well-lit retinal images.
  • Ensure the image is not blurry or obstructed.
  • Contact support if you face any issues.
